About IRC

Indian Rover Challenge is part of the Rover Challenge Series (RCS) of The Mars Society, USA which is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the human exploration settlement of the planet Mars. The Indian Rover Challenge features an engineering design challenge to engage students worldwide in the next phase of space exploration. Indian Rover Challenge is the only international robotics and space exploration competition of its kind in Asia-Pacific which aims to ignite and encourage the spirit of innovation amongst budding engineers. The competition challenges high school and college students to design and build next-generation Mars rovers and compete in Mars-simulated conditions.
